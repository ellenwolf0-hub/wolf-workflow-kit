# 60-Second Pitch

> Use this as your opener before running the demo.

---

"Every People team professional I know has the same problem: we do deeply human work — hiring, performance, org design, coaching — but we lose the thread constantly. Context lives in our heads, in old Slack threads, in transcripts nobody reads.

We built a workflow kit that fixes that. Three commands. Five minutes to install.

You start your day with `/orient` — it tells you what's open, what you were working on, what needs attention. You finish a calibration and run `/meet` — the decisions are captured, filed, and automatically sent to your team Slack. You close out with `/closeout` — tomorrow's pickups are already set.

The whole thing runs in your terminal, works with Granola and Slack out of the box, and doesn't require your teammates to change how they work. They get the output whether they use the kit or not.

Let me show you what it looks like."

---

**Timing guide:** Read this aloud at a normal pace — it's 45 seconds, leaving 15 seconds of buffer for pausing for effect.

**Key phrases to land:**
- "deeply human work" — grounds it in People team identity
- "three commands, five minutes to install" — low barrier
- "doesn't require your teammates to change how they work" — addresses adoption objection before it comes up
